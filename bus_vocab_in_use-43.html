Stress and Stress Management

Positive Work Feelings

  • Rewarding: Giving you satisfaction.
  • Stimulating: Interesting and making you feel good.
  • Stretched: Feeling that work can be difficult but in a good way.
  • Challenging: Difficult, but in an interesting and enjoyable way.

When Stimulation turns to Stress

  • Pressure building up: Increasing feelings of worry.
  • Overwhelmed by work: Feeling unable to do your work.
  • Under stress: Very worried about work.
  • Stress-induced: An illness caused by stress.
  • Stressed out: Extremely stressed due to overwork.
  • Nervous breakdown: A period of mental illness from worry, unable to sleep or work.
  • Burned out / Burnout: So tired by work you feel unable to work again.

Downshifting & Lifestyle

  • Rat race / Treadmill: The feeling that work is too competitive and relentless.
  • Quality of life: The standard of your health, comfort, and happiness.
  • Quality time: Time spent giving your full attention to someone.
  • Downshifting / Rebalancing: Choosing to work less and live a less stressful life.
